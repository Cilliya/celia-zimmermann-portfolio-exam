Group Submissions (03.01: Entrepreneurship & Ideation Week, 03.02: Business, Users & Communication, 03.03: Design Documentation & Usertesting, 03.04: The Prototype & Final Pitch)

We created the digital angency HINA and the digital service TROT. TROT is a travel app - a mix between Spotify and AirBnB. TROT and HINA are about creating, sharing and exploring.

Individual work

Throughout the four weeks the project lasted, my main role was to be one of the two designers in the group. I worked on the style tile and color scheme, and created the customer journeys. I drew the initial wireframe for the TROT app, and completed the Xd model.

Furthermore, I also did research for the design and identity of our digital agency website. I looked at various websites of digital agencies located in Copenhagen, and created an Xd model design for HINA based on inspiration and ideas gathered from these. I also worked on alternative designs for the logos, the topography graphics, among other things.
