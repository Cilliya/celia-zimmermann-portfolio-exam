Theme 02: Basic Animation

Individual Submissions (02.01: JavaScript Part 1, 02.02: Storytelling & Character design, 02.03: JavaScript Part 2)

We were introduced to JavaScript, storyboards, character design, colors, and how to work with obstructions. The obstructions were in the form of a particular film and genre, such the film noir Touch of Evil by Orson Welles.

I also worked with the art style of Motomichi Nakamura, which is the style my character Onaka, which I used in almost all of my individual submissions, is based on.

Individual work

I worked as a designer on the project. Art and story is first and foremost based on the film noir Touch of Evil and images of Venice Beach where it was filmed. However, I also based the art and storyboard on scenes from Batman: The Animated Series, and the video games Wolf Among Us and L.A. Noir.

I designed and created most of the backgrounds, and helped out with other creative tasks whenever needed. I worked in Photoshop and Illustrator, but also sketched on paper using pen and pencil.
