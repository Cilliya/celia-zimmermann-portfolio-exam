Group Submissions (03.02: City Walks and Meetup)

For the City Walks and Meetup project, we created a concept called "Two Lakes". A walk around the lakes situated in central Copenhagen followed by a beer at the Barking Dog bar.

We also created a simple website for our video. All projects and submissions were group submissions.

Individual Work

We were taught how to film, edit, direct, and produce our own video. We learned the importance of using a storyboard and creating a story, sound and music, as well as the equipment needed to produce the video.

I mainly worked on the script for the voice over, planning shots and filming. Our source of inspiration was Wes Anderson, and we wanted to create symmetric shots and include curious things from the area surrounding Sortedam Sø.
