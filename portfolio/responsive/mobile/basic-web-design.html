Theme 01: Basic Web Design

Individual Submissions (01.01: Content and Communication, 01.02: Basic HTML and CSS, 01.03: User Interface Design, 01.04: Responsive Web Design)

As this was the first theme, everything was new to me. I was introduced to Photoshop, Illustrator, HTML, and CSS.

My given style was Modernism, and I decided to base my design on Cubism. Minimalistic design inspired by the painting Transverse Beams by the artist Partick Henry Bruce, as seen on the frontpage of my responsive website.

Group Submissions

The task was to redesign a website. The website belonged to a restaurant named Martyr's Bay on the Isle of Iona. For this project, I worked with design and communication. I helped the scrum master and the rest of the design team get the planned work done in time.

For a business such as Martyr's Bay, it was important to keep it local and simple - authentic. We therefore decided to focus on the surrounding nature and the celtic history of the island. We used the original celtic logo, as well as real pictures taken by visitors and locals.
